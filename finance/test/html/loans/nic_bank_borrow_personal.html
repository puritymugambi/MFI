<h1>Loans</h1>
<p>Loan Types Available</p>
<ul>
<li>Personal  Unsecured Loans</li>
<li>Personal  Secured Loans (including plot purchase loans)</li>
<li>Car Loans</li>
<li>Home Loans</li>
<li>Home Loans Re-advance (Top Up)</li>
</ul>
<p>What are the requirements for our loan products?</p>
<ul>
<li>Age from between 21 55 years (or retirement age)</li>
<li>Employed/Regular Salary</li>
<li>Contract or Permanent &amp; Pensionable staff</li>
<li>Proof that your account income/salary has reflected in your account with NIC Bank for at least 3 months</li>
<li>Certified copy of ID</li>
<li>PIN Certificate</li>
<li>Latest three months pay slips (6 for commission staff)</li>
<li>Latest six months bank statements (12 for commission staff)</li>
<li>Letter from employer (NIC Bank format)</li>
<li>Check off letter (where applicable)</li>
<li>Latest utility bill OR lease agreement</li>
</ul>
